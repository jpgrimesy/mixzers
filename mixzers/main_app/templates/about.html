What is MixZers

MixZers is a way for college students to make money on their own time. .edu email addresses. We understand that finding a job as a student can be challenging with a busy schedule and barely any time for yourself, especially when you don't have a lot of work experience yet. That's why we've created a platform that connects students to jobs that suit their schedule and allows them to develop their job experience in the process, all while making some cash!

We've noticed navigting the job market online lacks trust and transparency. From Facebook Marketplace, to Craigslist, to LinkedIn, it's hard to know who you're really talking to, whether you are posting jobs or applying to them.


That being said... For students, Mixzers verifies your .edu email to give you the confidence to apply to any of the jobs knowing that the job poster knows you're a real person!
For job posters, our platform offers an easy and effective way to find and hire real people for odd jobs, temporary work, and more. By posting job opportunities on our platform, you'll be able to tap into a talented and motivated pool of college students who are eager to make some extra cash and gain valuable work experience.
