What is MixZers

MixZers is dedicated to helping college students find employment opportunities using their .edu email addresses. We understand that finding a job as a student can be challenging, especially when you don't have a lot of work experience yet. That's why we've created a platform that connects students with employers who are specifically looking to hire college students.

For employers, our platform offers an easy and effective way to find and hire college students for odd jobs, temporary work, and more. By posting job opportunities on our platform, you'll be able to tap into a talented and motivated pool of college students who are eager to make some extra cash and gain valuable work experience.
