What is MixZers?

MixZers is dedicated to helping college students find employment opportunities using their .edu email addresses. We understand that finding a job as a student can be challenging, especially when you don't have a lot of work experience yet. That's why we've created a platform that connects students with employers who are specifically looking to hire college students.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for students to find part-time or full-time work that fits their schedules and interests. We believe that college students have a lot to offer employers, and we want to help you showcase your skills and talents to potential employers.


Mixzers Team

Our team is made up of college students who understand the challenges of balancing academics and work. We're passionate about helping our fellow students succeed, and we're committed to providing a user-friendly app that makes it easy to search for jobs, apply for positions, and connect with employers.
